Suzy’s Fashion Moments from the Netflix K-Drama DOONA!

By Janice Chow | November 6, | 2023 3 mins read

From epic sagas drenched in mythology to relatable relationship themes, romantic K-Dramas never fail to deliver intriguing storylines that go beyond meet-cutes and dramatic love triangles. Released on Netflix on October 20, the trending Korean drama Doona! depicts an unconventional love story between a former K-pop idol and a fresh-faced college student.

Based on the popular webtoon The Girl Downstairs by Min Songa, Doona! stars Bae Suzy, better known as just Suzy, as Lee Doona who was once the linchpin of Korean girl group Dream Sweet. Burned out by the cutthroat world of K-pop, Doona drops out of the girl group and lives a low-key life in a share house at a college town. That’s where she crosses paths with Lee Won-jun (played by Yang Se-jong), a shy yet caring university student who lives upstairs in the same residence. Although the two characters initially get off on the wrong foot, their sharp and tense exchanges inevitably give way to a budding romance.

Suzy’s background as a real-life K-pop star combined with her ethereal visuals makes the artist a perfect cast for Doona. Another must-watch of the K-Drama – besides Suzy’s impeccable portrayal of the character − is her electrifying range of outfits showcasing a mix of onstage glam and off-duty chic. Read on for a deep dive into Suzy’s wardrobe from Doona!.

High-Octane Idol Get-Ups

Doona makes a killer impression in the first episode when she performs at a fully packed arena alongside other Dream Sweet members. In stark contrast with her groupmates’ black ensembles, Doona’s glamorous white co-ord set stands out with a midriff-baring design sassed up with gemstones and feathers – a reminder of the discord among members within the K-pop group. Glitzy accessories like a crystal-embellished choker and rhinestone studs add extra oomph to her stage look. Later, when Doona returns to the spotlight after her hiatus, she’s seen in unabashedly flashy pieces that radiate confidence including a pink tulle gown and mini dress with exaggerated puff sleeves.
